Who We Are

A Grassroots Organisation Built on Compassion

CARE FOR HUMANKIND (C4H) LIMITED (C4H) is a registered non-profit organisation dedicated to sustainable development, humanitarian relief, and social welfare in the most marginalised regions of Pakistan — with a particular focus on Tharparkar, one of the country's most climatically vulnerable areas.

We work directly with underserved Hindu and Muslim communities — including the Bheel, Mengar, Coli, Menghwar, and Soomro — to deliver lasting, dignified change.

Dignity First

Every intervention is designed to preserve and restore human dignity.

Sustainability

Solar-powered, low-maintenance solutions that outlast donor funding.

Inclusivity

We serve all communities regardless of faith, caste, or ethnicity.

Accountability

Field-documented evidence and transparent reporting for every project.

Focus Region

Why Tharparkar?

Tharparkar is Pakistan's only inhabited desert — and one of its most neglected regions. Chronic drought, extreme poverty, and marginalisation of minority communities create a compounded humanitarian crisis that demands sustained, compassionate intervention.

C4H operates directly on the ground in Chor Sub-District, building infrastructure and delivering services that governments and larger NGOs have long overlooked.
